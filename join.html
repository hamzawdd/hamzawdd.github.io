Want to join Barbell at Berkeley?

No experience required. If you decide to become a member of Barbell at Berkeley, you will get a chance to train in the Olympic Lifting Studio at the Recreational Sports Facility during Open Hours for a discounted rate of $85/semester. Open Hours will always be supervised by at least one of the team coaches who will be there to give you any lifting advice/coaching you may need. We will hold regular team training sessions which all members are expected to attend throughout the semester. Being a member of Barbell at Berkeley also means you will be able to compete at both local and national powerlifting/weightlifting competitions alongside your teammates!

Our info session will be held at 8PM on Thursday, September 1st in the Olympic Lifting Studio at the Recreational Sports Facility. Come see what we're all about and grab some free pizza!
